# Privacy Policy for Code Circulation

**Last Updated:** April 18, 2024

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Code Circulation ("we", "our", or "us") handles user privacy. This app is designed to operate without collecting any personal information from its users.

## Information Collection

**Code Circulation does not collect any personal data.** We do not:

- Request or store personal information
- Track your location
- Access your contacts or media
- Collect usage statistics
- Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
- Share any information with third parties

## Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect information from you.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
